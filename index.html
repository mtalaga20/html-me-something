Michael's Story in Hungary

The Train Ride

A suspenseful story of a brother and sister in a foreign country

By Michael Talaga (the subject of the story)

Brother and sister Michael and Lauren Talaga faced language barriers on a foreign train heading West for Austria and were left abandoned by their family on their vacation to Europe. Even worse, they got on the wrong train and had no money for tickets! Would they be arrested? Kicked off the one way train?

They boarded the train with their supposed tickets, which were actually for the next train. What happened next was any tourist's worst nightmare! A grumpy train conductor who spoke no English demanded tickets! "Ticket," she yelled, as her frustration grew increasingly as they struggled to come up with a solution. 30 minutes had passed, and the conductors started to swarm the siblings and were speaking with each other.

"I speak English," said one of her co-workers. "That'll be €200." How did they get out of that hole without any money? Luckily, the broke college kid was saved by the bell from a venmo transfer from his mother.
